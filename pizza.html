Margherita Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe is all about the best pizza that there is. The Margherita pizza. It's simple, but delicious. It requires only a few key ingredients, but that's the reason it's so good!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • mozzarella cheese (pearls or slices)
  • fresh basil leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Get the pizza oven nice and hot
  2. Prep the pizza dough by flattening it out and placing all of the toppings on it.
  3. Once the oven is nice and hot and there's a steady flame, place the pizza in the oven. Rotate every 30-60 seconds and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove the pizza from the oven and slice it right away to be served hot!
