Tacos Recipe

Description

These are what I would consider to be the best "street-style" tacos you can make at home. They're quick and simple, because they only require a few key ingredients. These tacos are perfect for cooking a meal for a large group of people. Everyone can grab a plate and a few taco shells and then pile on the ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of meat (beef, chicken, pork)
  • Corn Tortillas
  • White Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Limes
  • Cervesas

Steps

  1. Brown the meat and add seasoning while it cooks.
  2. While the meat is cooking, prep the onions and cilantro by cutting them both finely and placing them to the side.
  3. Once the meat is done cooking, cut/chop it into smaller pieces and warm the tortillas
  4. Serve the tacos with a beer of your choice. I'd go with a Modelo, but don't forget the lime!
